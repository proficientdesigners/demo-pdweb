    About Us

    “Education provides a wonderful platform for the growth and success of an individual with a positive impact on the society as well as the nation at large”. In 1960s, quotes like these inspired a lady residing in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, who envisioned a bright future for the young minds of India. She wanted to pave the way for every child in the community to acquire proper social values, leadership qualities and excellence in his/her respective field of study. This lady was Mrs.Buvana Krishnan, the founder of S.V.M and B.K.M Group of Schools.
