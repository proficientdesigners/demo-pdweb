What is Precast Tank?

Precast Tank is made by casting concrete using a mould in a controlled environment. This method of casting helps in creating a monolithic seamless tank with a joint-free structure which prevents any leakage and mixing of potable water with sewage and rainwater. These tanks are strong, require low maintenance, and help to store clean water and lasts for a lifetime.
